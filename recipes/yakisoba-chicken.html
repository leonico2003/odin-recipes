Yakisoba Chicken

Description

Learn to prepare yakisoba-chicken

You will love this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 tablespoon chile paste
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 onion sliced into eights
  • 1/2 medium cabbage head (chopped)
  • 2 carrots (chopped)
  • 8 ounces soba noodles (cooked and drained)

Steps

  1. Combine sesame oil, canola oil and chili paste, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  2. Add garlic and stir-fry another 30 seconds.
  3. Add the chicken and 1/4 cup of the soy sauce.
  4. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes.
  5. In the emptied pan combine onion, cabbage and carrots, stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in the remaining soy sauce, noodles and chicken to the pan.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
