Brownies

Description

Here you can learn how to prepare some delicious chocolate brownies with homemade frosting.

You will love this recipe!

Ingredients:

For the brownies

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup white Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking Powder

For the frosting

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confectioners sugar

Steps

    Making the brownies

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  3. Melt 1/2 cup in a saucepan.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  5. Add 1/3 cup cocoa powder, the flour, salt and baking powder.
  6. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake in the oven for about 25 to 30 minutes.
  8. Let it cool a bit before frosting.

    Making the frosting

  1. In a bowl,mix butter, confectioners sugar, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  2. Stir until it is smooth.
  3. Frost the brownies while they are warm.
