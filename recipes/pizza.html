Pizza

Description

Learn to prepare pizza

You will love this recipe!

Ingredients:

Pizza sauce

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 can Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano (crushed)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil (crushed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 envelopes of Pizza Crust Yeast or RapidRIse Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 1/3 cups of warm water
  • 1/3 cup oil

Toppings

  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees and grease 2 12-inch pizza pans.
  2. For the sauce, whisk together water, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. For the crust, combine 2 cups of flour, yeast, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  4. Add warm water, oil and mix until it's well blended.
  5. Add the remaining flour gradually until a soft and sticky dough it's formed.
  6. Divide the dough in half.
  7. Put each piece of dough into the pizza pans.
  8. Add the sauce, cheese and pepperoni.
  9. Bake in the oven until crust is brown and cheese is bubbly.
  10. Enjoy!
